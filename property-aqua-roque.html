DescriptionDescripción

From the principal terrace, the Atlantic runs to the horizon while the volcanic silhouette of Roque del Conde frames every sunrise — a view reserved for the few residences that hold this elevated position above Costa Adeje.

Drawn by HYLARQ on a 923 m² plot. Two floors, 553 m² built, 310 m² habitable. Upper floor: open living-dining-kitchen, sea-facing principal suite with dressing room and en-suite, covered pergola for year-round outdoor living, guest WC, utility. Lower floor: three further suites each with en-suite bathroom, and a multipurpose room configurable as cinema, gym or storage.

The infinity pool projects over the volcanic landscape. Private two-car garage. Delivered fully furnished and equipped — the architect's vision finished to the last detail. Ready to live in from day one.
