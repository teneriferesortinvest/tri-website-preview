DescriptionDescripción

Villa Nova is a private luxury residence in Adeje — a villa built for family life and entertaining, with a swimming pool, multiple terraces, an outside kitchen and a landscaped garden. The architecture opens the living spaces directly onto the terraces so that the boundary between inside and outside dissolves on a sunny afternoon.

The principal suite has a dedicated walk-in wardrobe and en-suite bathroom. Additional bedrooms and bathrooms serve the rest of the house. Kitchen and bathrooms are fully fitted with bespoke cabinetry. The villa is delivered fully furnished — ready to live in from day one.
