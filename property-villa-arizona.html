Villa Arizona is one of TRI's flagship equity projects. Drawn with Arizona in mind — long horizontals, warm stone, a house that refuses to compete with the land it sits on.

Five en-suite bedrooms, interior courtyard with a single olive tree, kitchen with a Sahara Noir marble slab for eight, infinity pool running the length of the south-facing garden.

Walk in with a suitcase. Every chair, every plate, every lamp was chosen for the household who would live here next. Not sold empty.
