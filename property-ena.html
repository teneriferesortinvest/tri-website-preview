About ENASobre ENA

ENA is positioned as "the luxury of experiencing the island from its most authentic side" — a residential sanctuary overlooking the Atlantic, surrounded by volcanic hills and natural light. 63 residences spread across nine floors, ranging from one-bedroom units at 48 m² to a single six-bedroom penthouse at 317 m² with 434 m² of terrace. Every unit has two garage spaces and a dedicated storage room.

Shared amenities elevate day-to-day living: a private infinity pool, a fully equipped gymnasium, a multi-purpose social space, and landscaped gardens that integrate the development with the volcanic landscape rather than sitting on top of it.

Developer: La Reserva Real Estate S.L.. Represented by Tenerife Resort Invest for international buyers. Completion targeted for mid-2027.

ENA se posiciona como *"el lujo de vivir la isla desde su lado más auténtico"* — un santuario residencial sobre el Atlántico, rodeado de colinas volcánicas y luz natural. 63 residencias repartidas en nueve plantas, desde apartamentos de un dormitorio de 48 m² hasta un único ático de seis dormitorios de 317 m² con 434 m² de terraza.
